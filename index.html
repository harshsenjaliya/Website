About Me

I'm a senior-year undergraduate student pursuing B.Tech. in Mechatronics engineering at Uka Tarsadia University, Bardoli, India.

Recently, I completed a MITACS Globalinked Research Internship (GRI) program in AIES Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ahmad Al-Mallahi at Dalhousie University, Truro, Canada. At AIES Lab, I worked on incredible research projects that use precision agriculture techniques to address issues facing the modern framing industry.

In past, I have worked with Dr. Pooja Shah from PDEU and Dr. Paresh Gujarati from CGPIT on the topic “Optimization of Rocker-Bogie Mechanism using Heuristic Approaches”. I also worked on a project entitled “A comparative study on the modern deep learning architectures for predicting nutritional deficiency in rice plants” under the supervision of Asst. Prof. Kirti Bardhan from NAU and Dr. Pooja Shah.
